In the fall 2014 after four years of consulting I was ready to transition my career off the road and into a career that married my passion for design thinking and business acumen. In 2015 I joined Yale School of Management (SOM) because of its integrated curriculum and focus on business and society. At SOM I’m focusing on strategy, marketing, and organizational design. I am also an officer in the Food and Wine and Entrepreneurship clubs and an interview lead in the Design and Innovation club.

My Favorite Assignment

My small team, cohorts are broken into teams of 8 for smaller projects. The first week at Yale School of Management is dedicated to course titles Managing Groups and Team. Small teams are provided a case where a sergeant has to ask the help of an disgruntle belligerent officer once again in front of his subordinates. Small groups are asked to write a response, then rank all the teams including theirs responses. Afterwards they come back into the room and there is a table in the middle with one seat for each team and row behind the seat. Leader can talk to the row but the row can only send notes. Leaders are asked to come to a decision.
